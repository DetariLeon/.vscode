About him

Kylian Mbappé Lottin is a world champion footballer of the French national team. Paris Saint-Germain striker. At the 2022 World Cup, he became only the second player in the history of the sport to score a hat-trick in the final.

On 24 March 2016, he made his debut for the France U19 national team in a 2016 UEFA European Under-19 Championship qualifier against Montenegro U19. Two days later, he scored his first goal against the Danish U19 national team.

He joined AS Monaco's academy in 2013. He made his league debut on 2 December 2015 in a 1–1 draw against Caen, coming on as a substitute for Fábio Coentrão in the 88th minute. By entering the field, he broke Thierry Henry's 21-year-old record, Kylian made his debut at 16 years and 347 days old. On 20 February 2016, he scored his first goal against Troyes at the age of 17 years and 62 days, again breaking Henry's record in the French club's history. In March, he signed his first professional contract with the club for 3 years.
